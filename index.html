Yonatan Oren

yonatano (at) cs.stanford.edu

I am a technologist based in the San Francisco Bay Area. I am interested in machine learning, statistics, and large language models.

I earned a B.Sc. in Mathematics in 2020 and a M.Sc. in Computer Science in 2023 from Stanford University.

At Stanford, I worked with Tatsu Hashimoto on language modeling under distribution shift and retrieval-augmented language models for structured prediction and code generation. More recently, I have been working on developing statistical methods for studying memorization in large language models.

In graduate school, I served as a teaching assistant for courses in probability and statistics. During undergrad, I helped run TreeHacks.
